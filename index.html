Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

1931 – 2015

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was one of the most admired scientists and leaders of India. He played a vital role in India’s space and missile development programs and inspired millions of students with his vision and humility.

What I admire most about him is his simplicity, dedication to education, and love for the youth of the nation. Despite achieving great success, he remained grounded and always encouraged young minds to dream big.

His life teaches us that with hard work, discipline, and honesty, anyone can rise from humble beginnings to achieve greatness.

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”
